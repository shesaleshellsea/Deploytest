Nakhonratchasima

29.12.2019-1.01.2020

This trip I go with my middle school friend we are appointment on new year 2019-2020 to party to countdown to new year. We go by 4 personal car with 14 people.We set off from morning of 29th december 2019.Because we afraid that there not enough space for pitch a tent. That when we are arrived at Pha Kluai Mai it has a lot of space for us to set up a tent.We set up a tent finished around afternoon then we relax and sleep on camp to evening. Then we cook BBQ to eat in dinner.

Next day we travel in National park Kho yai and we explore around our camp.Day 3 we have to go down from Kao Yai into the city to buy ingredients and meat to cook during waiting to countdown. After we shopping along the wat to back to camp we drive until meet the big wild elephants.Then we cooking until countdown.Last day we go back to home safely.
